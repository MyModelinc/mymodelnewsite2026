About mymodel

mymodel is an audience intelligence platform that rebuilds the relationship between brands and consumers from the ground up. Users build a 360-degree behavioral Stats Card — a digital profile they own completely. Brands search for and deploy Audience Orders into matching behavioral segments, paying users directly for permission to reach them.

Unlike data brokers or ad networks that extract and resell consumer data without consent or compensation, mymodel makes the payment the consent. Every transaction is per-offer, explicit, and 100% of the payment goes to the user. mymodel earns through membership and transaction fees — additive to, not taken from, user earnings.

The result is the first audience intelligence system where the quality signal is verified by the financial commitment of the consumer: people who accept an Audience Order are real, motivated, and self-identified.
